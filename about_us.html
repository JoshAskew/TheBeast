About Us

The Beast Hockey Club was founded in 2023 by Ian Bing and Cody Fries, who shared a vision for a program dedicated to skill development through positive reinforcement. From our inception, our goal has been to create an environment where players can grow and thrive both on and off the ice. Thanks to the support of fantastic families and valuable partnerships, we’ve been able to expand our teams and strengthen our community. We’re excited to have you join the Beast family and look forward to achieving great things together!
