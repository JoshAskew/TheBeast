WHO ARE WE

The Beast Hockey Club was established by Ian Bing and Cody Fries in 2023 with a mission to create a positive environment for hockey players. Our focus is on developing skills in skating and stick handling, while most importantly, fostering the growth of players as outstanding teammates.

We begin practices in late March or early April, depending on the team. The youngest team starts soon after the association season ends to allow more time for learning the rules of hockey, while the older teams start at the beginning of April.

Each year, we participate in four tournaments scheduled for May, June, August, and September. This timing gives us April to regroup and have some scrimmages before the tournament season. Three of the tournaments are local, while one is a travel tournament. We aim to have all our teams compete in the same tournaments.
